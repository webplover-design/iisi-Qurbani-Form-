Qurbani Help

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about online booking, Qurbani selection, payments, and meat distribution.

By what method does the Institute handle the Qurbani process?

The Institute arranges Qurbani through trusted supervision, records each booking, completes the sacrifice according to Islamic requirements, and manages the meat according to the option selected by the donor.

How can I choose the specific animal for my Qurbani act?

You can choose from the available packages on the booking form, such as Cow Per Share, Full Cow, or Goat. The Institute then manages the arrangement according to your selected package.

What is the way to distribute Qurbani donations?

If you select Waqf or donation, the sacrificial meat is used for resident students of the Institute and other needy or deserving people.

Can people overseas participate in Qurbani?

Yes. Overseas participants can book online, review the amount in available currencies, and share their payment receipt through WhatsApp for confirmation.

How much does a cow share cost for Qurbani?

Cow Per Share pricing depends on the selected option. Waqf bookings can select PKR 30,000 or PKR 35,000, while Collection bookings are priced at PKR 35,000.

How will I receive confirmation after booking?

After submitting your booking, you will receive a tracking token. Your booking is confirmed after the Institute receives your payment receipt and verifies the details.

Can I book more than one Qurbani item?

Yes. Use the “Choose Another Animal” option during booking to add multiple Qurbani items before submitting your final order.

What should I do after making payment?

After transferring the amount, share your payment receipt on WhatsApp using the link shown on the token page so the Institute can confirm your booking.
